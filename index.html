About me

I'm a third year PhD student in the Center of Vision, Speech and Signal Processing at the University of Surrey (UK) supervised by Prof. Adrian Hilton and Dr. Charles Malleson. I am currently focusing on the 3D reconstruction of animals from monocular views. My research interests include 3D reconstruction, computer vision, computer graphics, deep learning, visual effects, and real-time technology.
