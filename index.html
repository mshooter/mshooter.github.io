Biography

I'm a first year PhD student in the Center of Vision, Speech and Signal Processing at the University of Surrey (UK) under the supervision of Prof. Adrian Hilton and Dr. Charles Malleson. My research interests include computer vision and graphics, machine learning and 3D reconstruction.

Currently my research involves investigating deep learning methods for non-contact measurement and generation of 3D animal motion and appearance.

In addition to doing research I love real-time technology and animation, reading, playing music, nature and learning new things!
